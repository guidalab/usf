Active Members

Wayne Guida, PhD

Dr. Wayne Guida earned his Bachelor of Science in Chemistry from the University of South Florida, followed by a PhD in Organic Chemistry from the University of Florida.

His current research focuses on computational drug design and structure-based modeling, specifically investigating kinase inhibitors and protein-ligand interactions to develop targeted therapies for stimulator of interferon genes (STING) and nicotinamide phosphoribosyltransferase (NAMPT). Dr. Guida’s interdisciplinary background bridges organic chemistry, computational biology, and pharmacology, allowing him to approach biomedical challenges from multiple perspectives to advance therapeutic innovation.
